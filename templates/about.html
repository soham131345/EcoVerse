EcoVerse

A Comprehensive Sustainability & Climate Action Platform

Created for ICS Competition 2025/2026

Project Overview

EcoVerse is an interactive, gamified platform designed to educate users about climate change, sustainability, and environmental responsibility. Through engaging modules like carbon tracking, recycling education, green token economics, and climate simulations, EcoVerse makes environmental awareness accessible and actionable for everyone.

Project Goals

  • Raise climate change awareness through interactive experiences
  • Gamify sustainability education for better engagement
  • Provide actionable tools for reducing carbon footprint
  • Create an inclusive platform for all age groups

Key Features

  • Real-time carbon footprint calculator
  • AI-powered recycling assistant
  • Aviation carbon offset simulation
  • EcoWorld city builder game
  • Sustainability certification system

Supported UN Sustainable Development Goals

EcoVerse directly contributes to four key United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), addressing critical global challenges through technology and education.

7

SDG 7: Affordable Clean Energy

EcoVerse promotes renewable energy through its EcoWorld city builder where users create solar farms and wind turbines, and educates about clean energy transition in climate modules.

Solar Energy Education Renewable Investment
12

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption & Production

The AI Recycling Assistant teaches proper waste management, while carbon tracking encourages mindful consumption patterns and sustainable lifestyle choices.

Recycling Education Sustainable Consumption Waste Reduction
13

SDG 13: Climate Action

Directly addressed through climate timeline visualization, carbon footprint tracking, aviation offset simulations, and personalized action recommendations to combat climate change.

Carbon Tracking Climate Education Emission Reduction
15

SDG 15: Life on Land

EcoWorld's tree planting mechanics, biodiversity tracking, and ecosystem preservation features teach users about terrestrial ecosystem conservation and reforestation importance.

Tree Planting Biodiversity Ecosystem Protection
