CARBON DASHBOARD TUTORIAL
Welcome to Carbon Dashboard!
This dashboard helps you track and reduce your carbon footprint. Here's what you can do:

1. Carbon Calculator: Measure your environmental impact
2. Flight Simulator: Learn about aviation emissions through interactive flights
3. Carbon Budget: Track your daily CO₂ allowance
4. Certification: Earn badges for sustainable actions
🌱
Using the Carbon Calculator
The Carbon Calculator analyzes your lifestyle in 5 steps:

Step 1: Transportation - How you commute daily
Step 2: Home Energy - Your electricity and heating usage
Step 3: Diet - What you eat and food waste
Step 4: Consumption - Shopping habits and electronics
Step 5: Travel - Flight frequency

Each selection affects your carbon footprint calculation. Be honest for accurate results!
📊
Understanding Your Results
After completing the calculator, you'll see:

Annual Footprint: Your total CO₂ emissions in kilograms
Daily Budget: Recommended daily CO₂ limit
Breakdown: Emissions by category (Transportation, Home, Food, Consumption)
AI Recommendations: Personalized tips to reduce your footprint

You'll earn Eco Score points for completing the analysis!
🎯
Flight Simulator Guide
The Flight Simulator teaches about aviation emissions:

1. Book a Flight: Choose route, airline, aircraft, and seat
2. Complete Immigration: Passport control simulation
3. Flight Animation: Real-time flight simulation
4. Carbon Quiz: Test your knowledge about aviation emissions
5. Earn Tokens: Get rewarded for correct quiz answers

Daily Limit: 2 flights per day to encourage sustainable travel
✈️
Certification & Rewards

How to earn points:
• Complete carbon calculator
• Complete flights
• Correct quiz answers (+tokens)
