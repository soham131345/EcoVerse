Recycling Tips & Best Practices

Do Recycle

  • ✓ Clean plastic bottles & containers
  • ✓ Paper & cardboard (dry)
  • ✓ Glass bottles & jars
  • ✓ Aluminum & steel cans
  • ✓ Clean food containers

Don't Recycle

  • ✗ Plastic bags (take to store)
  • ✗ Pizza boxes with grease
  • ✗ Broken glass or mirrors
  • ✗ Styrofoam containers
  • ✗ Batteries & electronics

Pro Tips

  • Rinse containers to remove food residue before recycling
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space in recycling bins
  • Keep plastic caps ON bottles - they're recyclable too!
  • Check local recycling guidelines - rules vary by location
